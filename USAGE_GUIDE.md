# CSR Scholarship Dashboard - Usage Guide

## Getting Started

The dashboard is now running at **http://localhost:5174/**

## Three Main Sections

### 1. Browse Scholarships Tab
This is where you can explore all 20 CSR scholarships.

**Features:**
- **Search Bar**: Type to search by scholarship name, organization, or description
- **Filters**: 
  - Country dropdown (currently all India)
  - Grade Level (High School, Undergraduate, Graduate, Vocational)
  - Min/Max Amount filters (in INR)
- **Sorting**: Click any column header to sort:
  - Scholarship name
  - Organization
  - Amount
  - Grade level
  - Deadline
- **Clear Filters**: Remove all filters at once

### 2. Match Me Tab
Get personalized scholarship recommendations!

**How to Use:**
1. Fill out the form with your information:
   - **Grade Level**: Your current education level
   - **Field of Study**: Your major (e.g., Engineering, Medicine)
   - **GPA**: Your grade point average (0-4.0 scale)
   - **Family Income**: Annual household income in INR
   - **Country**: Your location

2. Click "Find My Scholarships"

3. The system will:
   - Calculate compatibility scores for each scholarship
   - Show only scholarships with 40%+ match
   - Display results sorted by match score
   - Add match percentage badges to each scholarship

**Match Score Breakdown:**
- 25 points: Grade level match
- 30 points: Field of study alignment
- 20 points: GPA requirements
- 25 points: Family income eligibility
- Bonus points for exceeding requirements

### 3. Analytics Tab
Visual insights into scholarship data.

**Charts Available:**
1. **Average Amount by Grade**: Bar chart showing funding by education level
2. **Grade Distribution**: Pie chart of scholarship distribution
3. **Amount Distribution**: How scholarships are distributed across price ranges
4. **Income Eligibility**: Line chart showing eligibility by family income
5. **Top Organizations**: Which companies offer the most scholarships
6. **Popular Fields**: Most common fields of study

**Key Statistics:**
- Total scholarships available
- Average scholarship amount
- Maximum funding available
- Number of organizations
- Highest scholarship details
- Most common grade level
- Total funding pool
- Average GPA requirement

## Tips for Best Results

### For Students:
1. **Be Accurate**: Enter your real information for best matches
2. **Be Flexible**: Consider scholarships slightly outside your exact field
3. **Check Deadlines**: Sort by deadline to see what's closing soon
4. **Read Requirements**: Click "Apply" to visit the scholarship website

### For Analysis:
1. **Compare Organizations**: See which companies focus on specific fields
2. **Find Your Range**: Use amount filters to find scholarships you qualify for
3. **Identify Opportunities**: Analytics show under-served areas
4. **Plan Ahead**: Use deadline data to organize applications
